Burn Permits

Please visit the Michigan DNR website to see if open burning is allowed in your township today. You must do this every time you wish to burn vegetation or leaves. Burning when not allowed, or openly burning prohibited items like building materials, could result in a citation. If you cause a forest fire, you could be liable for all the costs of that fire.

About

Sauble-Elk-Eden Townships Fire Department (SEE FD, "Irons Fire Department") is located in Lake County, Michigan. The department is staffed with paid-on-call firefighters and provides fire, medical, and rescue services to its three township coverage area. SEE FD provides mutual aid to surrounding jurisdictions as well. The new central fire station on West 8 Mile Road was opened in 2022 and replaces the old Station 1 and 2. Seven pieces of apparatus are housed at the central station, including two engines, two tenders, mini pumper, brush truck, and a quick response vehicle.

Services

  • Fire suppression
  • Medical first response
  • Vehicle extrication
  • Ice water rescue
  • Alarm, odor, and smoke investigation
  • Pre-incident planning
  • Public education
